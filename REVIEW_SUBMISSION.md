## SECTION 1: Mandatory Criteria Processing Errors

### Error 1.1: Wrong Criteria Standard Applied (Talent vs Promise)

**Assessor's Statement:** _"The mandatory references speak well of the candidate, but do not explain how they are one of the world's leading talents in technology..."_

**Processing Error:** The assessor applied the **Exceptional Talent** standard to my **Exceptional Promise** application.

The Tech Nation guide defines the Mandatory Criteria differently for each route:

| Route                   | Mandatory Criteria Requirement                                                    |
| ----------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Exceptional Talent**  | _"show that they have been recognised as a **leading talent**"_                   |
| **Exceptional Promise** | _"show they have been recognised as having **potential to be** a leading talent"_ |

The assessor's criticism that the letters do not explain how I am "one of the world's leading talents" applies the Talent standard. For Exceptional Promise, I need only demonstrate **potential** for future leadership - which my referees explicitly address.

This misapplication of criteria is a fundamental processing error that affected the entire assessment of my Mandatory Criteria evidence.
